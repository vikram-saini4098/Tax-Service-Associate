Steps to track e-filing status


Get a complete visibility around the lifecycle of your tax return.                                          



  1. Login to your account

    Once your filing is complete, you can track the filing status.


  2. Click on my tax returns

    Go to the entity page where you will see all the tax returns filed.


  3. Know your income tax returns

    Click on "Check Status" and view the complete lifecycle status.
